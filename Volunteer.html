Frequently Asked Questions

How much time do I need to commit?

We have opportunities with varying time commitments, from one-time events to regular weekly shifts. Most volunteers contribute 4-8 hours per month.

Do I need special skills or experience?

Most positions don't require special skills - we provide training. Some roles (like teaching dance) may require relevant experience.

Can I volunteer as a group?

Yes! We welcome corporate groups, student organizations, and other teams. Contact us to arrange group volunteering opportunities.

Is there an age requirement?

Most opportunities are for ages 16+. We have some youth programs for ages 12-15 with parental supervision.

How do I get started?

Simply click the "Sign Up" button on any opportunity that interests you, or contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteers@thechairly.org.
