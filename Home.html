Our Vision

Mthombo’ngashi NPO envisions a thriving, equitable society where every individual has access to nutritious food, quality education, comprehensive healthcare, and the transformative power of art and music. We strive to foster justice and equality by empowering marginalized communities, breaking systemic barriers, and nurturing sustainable development. Through innovative programs and collaborative partnerships, we aim to build resilient communities where dignity, opportunity, and creativity flourish for all..

Objective

To collect food donations from local shops and businesses.

To distribe donated items to individuals and families in need.

To raise awareness about food insecurity and encourage community involvement in addressing this issue.

To close the gap in education to those who are dis-privilege by providing free education.

To provide health care services to those who cannot access health care easiestly.

To challenge and educate about equality and justice.

Core Value

Mthombo'ongashi our core values are compassion, Integrity, transparent, Accountability, and Community drive all our decisions and actions.

At Mthombo’ngashi NPO, our work is guided by the following fundamental principles, which reflect our commitment to sustainable development and human dignity:

1. Equity & Justice

2. Empowerment Through Knowledge

3. Holistic Well-being

4. Sustainable Food Sovereignty

5. Integrity & Accountability

Our Mission

Mthombo'ongashi is committed to eradicating hunger and promoting food security, education, health care service, art & music and equality in times of crisis in our community through compassionate action and community support.

Mthombo’ngashi NPO is committed to empowering communities through integrated, sustainable solutions in food security, education, healthcare, justice, and the arts. We work tirelessly to alleviate hunger, expand access to quality education, improve health outcomes, advocate for social justice, and harness the transformative power of art and music for community development. we strive to create a just and equitable society where every individual has the opportunity to thrive."

We address interconnected challenges through comprehensive community development.
