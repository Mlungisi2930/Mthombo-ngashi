Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, we are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your records.

How will my donation be used?

85% of all donations go directly to program services, 10% to administrative costs, and 5% to fundraising. Our annual report provides detailed financial breakdowns.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes! Through PayPal you can set up monthly donations. For bank transfers, you can arrange standing orders with your bank.

Do you accept stock donations?

Yes, we accept stock and securities donations. Please contact our finance team at donations@example.com for transfer instructions.

How can I get a receipt for my donation?

PayPal donations receive automatic receipts. For bank transfers, email donations@example.com with your transfer details to request a receipt.
